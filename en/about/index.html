📌 Industrial Designer

💘 Illustrator

🧩 Programmer

🙈 Human

Caper
AsDevActus

I began studying web programming out of curiosity, but I soon found myself immersed in the world of web development. For over two years, I have been learning both on the platform and independently. This experience has revealed the vast possibilities that programming offers and has fueled my desire to continue learning.

I have participated in short group projects coordinated by NoCountry, where I gained valuable experience. These simulations taught me to view web development in a more systematic way.

As a web developer, my aspirations are to gain enough experience and knowledge to transform ideas into products in an agile and efficient manner. I am committed to sharing my learnings with others and helping to grow the community, just as it has supported me.

As a child, I enjoyed drawing my favorite characters. This passion has continued over the years, and through self-study and learning, I have reached a level where I feel confident in my own creations.

Drawing is an integral part of my identity, and I will never stop doing it. I strive to improve continually so that I can better convey my ideas through this medium.

I enjoy digital illustration because of its convenience, however, painting with colored pencils or acrylics and oils has a unique charm that cannot be compared. I hope to inspire others to draw, even in an era where generative AI threatens the craft of illustration.
